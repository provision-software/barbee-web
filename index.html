About

What is BarBee?

BarBee is an app to connect bars with experienced bartenders when they need help quickly. It is the uber and tinder of bars and restaurants.

Bar owners, have you ever had someone not show up for their shift and you call in the backup only for them to arrive after the lunch rush is already over? What if you could open an app and see multiple qualified bartenders within a few blocks of you who could be there in mere minutes?

Bartenders, with the gig economy thriving and your skills, wouldn’t it be nice if you could make some extra cash on the side?

BarBee aims to connect bars in need with available bartenders and fill a space that has been lacking within the gig economy.
